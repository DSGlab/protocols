Modified TENS Miniprep

   *** MAKE FRESH TENS BUFFER EVERY TIME YOU PREP ***

Reagents:
   Fresh TENS Buffer ( 0.1 N NaOH and 0.5% SDS in T10E1 )
   Cold 3.0 M KOAc ( 3.0 M KOAc (pH ≤ 5.2)
   Cold 100% ethanol
   Cold 70% ethanol 
   TE
   RNase

To make TENS buffer for 10 plasmid preps:
   100 µL 20% SDS
   40 µL 10N NaOH
   3.86 mL T10E1 (pH 7.5)

To make TE:
   10 mL 1M Tris (Ph 7.5)
   0.2 mL 0.5M EDTA
   89.8 µL H2O


Protocol:
1. Spin 1 mL overnight culture at 4,000 rpm for 5 min.
2. Remove supernatant.
3. Add 30 µL TE and vortex 2-5 sec to resuspend the pellet.
4. Add 300 µL TENS buffer. Gently invert to mix (shaking too hard will result in isolation of chromosomal DNA).
5. Add 150 µL cold 3.0 M KOAc (pH ≤ 5.2). Invert to mix.
6. Spin at 13,000 rpm for 10 min at RT to pellet proteins.
7. Transfer supernatant to clean tube. Spin again.
8. Trandfer supernatant to clean tube. Add 1 mL cold 100% ethanol and invert to mix.
9. Spin at 13,000 rpm for 10 min at RT to pellet DNA.
10. Discard supernatant and add 1 mL cold 70% ethanol. Spin for 5 min and repeat wash again. Air dry for 1 hour.
11.Resuspend in 30 µL TE. Add 0.5 µg RNase A (0.5 µL of 19mg/mL stock) and incubate at 37ºC for 1 hour.
12. Run 1-2 µL on gel to check.
